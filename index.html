About Quality Education

Quality Education is one of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Our mission is to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Our Goals

  • Universal Access: Ensure that all girls and boys complete free, equitable, and quality primary and secondary education.
  • Early Childhood Development: Ensure access to quality early childhood development, care, and pre-primary education.
  • Skills for Work: Increase the number of youth and adults with relevant skills for employment, decent jobs, and entrepreneurship.
  • Eliminate Disparities: Eliminate gender disparities in education and ensure equal access to all levels of education and vocational training
  • Quality Education: Improve the quality of education and learning outcomes by ensuring a safe, non-violent, inclusive, and effective learning environment for all.

Take Action

Join us in the fight for quality education for all. Here's how you can help:

  • Donate to Education Programs: Support organizations working towards providing quality education for all.
  • Volunteer as a Tutor or Mentor: Help students achieve their educational goals by offering your time and knowledge.
  • Advocate for Education Policies: Advocate for policies that support inclusive and equitable quality education.
